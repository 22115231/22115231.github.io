Unit 8

Unit 8 introduced me to the world of mobile app development using the MIT App Inventor platform. Throughout this unit, I gained practical, hands-on experience in building apps from the ground up, integrating various interactive elements. A standout project was the development of an educational app specifically tailored for 7-year-old students to aid them in learning the alphabet. This app incorporated engaging activities, games, and exercises designed to make the process of learning the alphabet an enjoyable and immersive experience for young learners.

This project allowed me to combine my newly acquired app development skills with a focus on creating educational tools specifically designed for the needs and interests of young children. Overall, Unit 8 provided me with a solid foundation in mobile app development and the opportunity to create a practical and meaningful application aimed at fostering early literacy skills in a fun and interactive manner.
