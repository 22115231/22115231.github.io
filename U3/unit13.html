Unit 13

Unit 13 revolved around the realm of web development, where I acquired invaluable experience working with the triumvirate of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. HTML served as the backbone, enabling me to structure and organize content on web pages in a logical and semantic manner. CSS, the styling powerhouse, granted me the ability to design visually captivating websites, with precise control over layout, color schemes, and typography. JavaScript, the language of interactivity, empowered me to breathe life into my web projects, adding dynamic behavior and engaging user experiences.

Through practical assignments and hands-on projects, I embarked on the journey of building websites from the ground up, applying my newfound knowledge of these core web technologies. Unit 13 provided me with a solid foundation in the fundamentals of web development, equipping me with the necessary skills and understanding to further explore and excel in this ever-evolving field.
