IPhones

Apple's iPhone 15 lineup takes smartphones to a new level of performance and efficiency. The latest A16 Bionic processor for regular models and even more powerful A17 Bionic for Pro versions will ensure ultra-fast processing of any tasks.

OLED displays in all iPhone 15 reproduce incredibly bright, realistic colors and smooth HDR content. Pro models feature ProMotion dynamic refresh technology up to 120Hz for even smoother animations and scrolling. And the built-in always-on display mode allows you to view important information even on the locked screen.

The first-class camera system with ProRes optics and incredibly detailed 48MP shooting capabilities in Pro models will allow you to take professional photos and videos. And the autonomy of all iPhone 15 reaches record levels thanks to the optimization of iOS 16 and new batteries.

About IPhone

The key upgrades are improvements in performance, cameras, displays and battery life compared to the iPhone 14.

Display:
  • iPhone 15 Pro models received an always-on display that constantly displays the time, widgets and information when the screen is locked.
Processor
  • The iPhone 15 is equipped with the latest A16 Bionic chip, and the Pro models are equipped with an even more powerful A17 chipset, which is 30% faster than the previous one.
Cell:
  • Pro models are equipped with a main camera with a resolution of 48 MP, which allows you to take photos with incredible detail.
Charge and autonomy:
  • Battery capacity has increased in all iPhone 15 models. Battery life has increased by several hours.
