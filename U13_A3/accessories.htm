Accessories

Power banks - compact and powerful power sources for recharging smartphones, tablets and even laptops while traveling or far from an outlet - have become an integral part of the gadgets of the era of mobility. Capacitive batteries in combination with modern fast charging technologies make power banks indispensable devices for all connoisseurs of mobile devices.

The assortment of our catalog includes compact MagSafe compatible models from 5000 mAh for iPhone, high-performance 25600 mAh giants with several USB outputs for simultaneous charging of a whole group of equipment, as well as convenient pocket options with a capacity of 20000 mAh with LED flashlight and indicator of the remaining charge .
