Xiaomi

Xiaomi 13, 13 Pro and 13 Ultra continue Xiaomi's tradition of creating powerful flagships at an affordable price. The latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 provides speed at the level of the best Android smartphones.

The displays in the Xiaomi 13 series are distinguished by some of the best colors and contrast among smartphones, and the 120 Hz refresh rate makes the animation as smooth as possible. Triple and dual Leica cameras with unique modes allow you to create high-quality photos and videos.

And the compact and stylish Xiaomi 13 Lite based on Snapdragon 7 Gen 1 will be a worthy alternative to flagships for those who do not need maximum performance. Battery charge is enough for a full day of active use.
