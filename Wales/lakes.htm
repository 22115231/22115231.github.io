Lakes

North Wales has many beautiful lakes and rivers, including Llyn Tegid, near Bala, which is the largest natural lake in Wales.
Other lakes include Llyn Ogwen at the foot of Tryfan, Llyn Llydaw, near Snowdon (the legendary home of King Arthur's "Lady of the Lake"),
Llyn Padarn and Llyn Peris, near Lianberis, Llyn Elsi above Betws y Coed and many reservoirs including
Lyn Cowlyd, Llyn Celyn (aka. Tryweryn), Lake Vyrnwy and Marchlyn Mawr
